How to Register ?
 

Ops Hackathon site is not yet SSO compatible. Please follow this steps to register.
1. Provide your PayPal corp emailId, any password( atleast 6 characters) and click on "Register" button.
2. A verification e-mail wil be sent to you, please follow the e-mail instructions to verify yourself and login.
3. Once you are verified and logged in you can create teams, provide information about T-shirt size.
